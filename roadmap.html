MASQ Roadmap

Solana Wallet Integration

Implement seamless Solana wallet login for user authentication, enabling secure and decentralized access to the MASQ platform. Players can connect their Solana wallets to manage in-game assets and transactions. (Currently wallet integration is already available for buying packs but not for logins)

Online multiplayer PvP gameplay

Launch real-time online PvP battles where players match against global opponents using their customized decks. Introduce ranked ladders, seasonal rewards, and matchmaking algorithms to ensure fair and competitive gameplay.

$MASQ assets marketplace

Integrate a marketplace for trading MASQ assets on the Solana blockchain, ensuring transparency and security. More assets related to the game to be rolled out.

Enhanced Animations & Sounds

Introduce dynamic card animations, immersive sound effects, and particle effects for in-game actions. Enhance the visual and auditory experience to create a more engaging gameplay environment.

Mobile App Development

Launch MASQ as a full-fledged mobile game on the iOS App Store and Google Play Store. Optimize the game for mobile devices, including touch controls, offline play, and cross-platform synchronization.

Competitive Tournaments & Leaderboards

Introduce global tournaments, ranked leaderboards, and seasonal events. Offer $MASQ rewards for top players, fostering a competitive community and increasing player engagement.

Expanded Game Modes & Story

Add new game modes, including single-player campaigns and co-op challenges. Develop a rich lore and narrative to deepen the MASQ universe, with player-driven story events.
