3. Gameplay Overview

Turn-based battles against Grok, the AI opponent. Cards have attack, health, and abilities. Mana grows each turn. Win by reducing your opponent’s health to zero.

3.1 Combat Mechanics

Players draw cards, play cards, and engage in combat each turn. Mana growth ensures a natural escalation of power. Card types include:

  • Attackers: Deal damage directly to enemies.
  • Defenders: High-health cards that shield your deck.
  • Utility: Manipulate the board, mana, or card draws.

3.2 Field Placement & Strategy

Cards are placed on the board in lines of attack and defense, requiring strategic planning to break through or hold the line.

3.3 Combo System

Combos are at the heart of MASQuerades, rewarding players who can chain synergistic cards together:

  • Trigger Abilities: Bonus effects activated by previous cards.
  • Chain Effects: Stackable bonuses like mana refunds, bonus draws, or damage boosts.
  • Adaptive Play: Success forces opponents to rethink their approach.

Example Combo:

  • Mana Surge Initiator: Boost mana this turn.
  • Whispers of the Ancients: Draw 2 cards if you boosted mana.
  • Overload Blast: Spend extra mana to deal massive damage.
